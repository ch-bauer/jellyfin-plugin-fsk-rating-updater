Seasons and episodes use the series certification from TMDb and otherwise inherit the rating from their parent. Requires "Update TV series".
When off, an existing FSK rating is only reformatted (e.g. "FSK 12" → "FSK-12"). When on, TMDb is asked again and may overwrite it.
When TMDb has no German (FSK) certification but another country lists a numeric age rating (e.g. Russian/Nordic "12+"), map it to the matching FSK level. Only values that line up with a valid FSK level (0/6/12/16/18) are used; foreign letter ratings (PG-13, R) are ignored here.
